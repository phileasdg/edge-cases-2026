💡 Tip: High-Res Retina Export

For perfect pixel-accurate graphics (e.g. 2x/3x Retina resolution for publications):

1. Right-click the preview card and select Inspect.
2. Press Cmd+Shift+P (macOS) or Ctrl+Shift+P (Windows) to open the Command Menu.
3. Type Capture node screenshot and hit enter. Your browser will download a pristine full-resolution PNG.

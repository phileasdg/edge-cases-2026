The Concept

While network science is inherently interdisciplinary, research in the field is often siloed by speciality or domain. Edge Cases is a satellite event at CCS 2026 aiming to bridge these silos by showcasing the diversity and flexible applicability of network science methods. By highlighting research applying network science to unconventional domains, we aim to encourage cross-pollination across less connected problem spaces.

The Concept

While network science is inherently interdisciplinary, academic structures often silos researchers by domain. Edge Cases is a satellite symposium designed to break these boundaries, highlighting the diversity and universality of network science applications.

We highlight research applying network science to unconventional domains, exploring how shared computational frameworks reveal unique insights when applied across diverse and distinct contexts.

Organized by Complexity Coffee (the SFI complexity global school alumni network), this event prioritizes the novel, the computational, and the interdisciplinary.

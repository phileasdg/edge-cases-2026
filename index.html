The Concept

While network science is inherently interdisciplinary, research in the field is often siloed by speciality or domain. Edge Cases is a proposed satellite event aiming to bridge these silos by showcasing the diversity and flexible applicability of network science methods. By highlighting research applying network science to unconventional domains, we aim to encourage cross-pollination across less connected problem spaces.
